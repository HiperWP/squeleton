THE TRANSFORMATION

Walter White, a struggling high school chemistry teacher, is diagnosed with inoperable lung cancer. To secure his family's financial future, he teams up with former student Jesse Pinkman to produce and sell crystal methamphetamine.

What starts as a desperate act transforms into a dark journey of power, pride, and moral decay. Watch as Mr. White becomes Heisenberg—the most dangerous man in the Southwest.

THE JOURNEY

SEASON 1: THE BEGINNING

Walter White discovers he has terminal cancer and decides to cook meth with former student Jesse Pinkman. The partnership begins, and so does the descent.

SEASON 2: COMPLICATIONS

The operation expands. Walter and Jesse face dangerous dealers, personal tragedies, and the consequences of their actions begin to mount.

SEASON 3: THE EMPIRE

Enter Gus Fring and the superlab. Walter's transformation accelerates as he becomes more ruthless and his alter ego Heisenberg takes control.

SEASON 4: THE WAR

The battle between Walter and Gus escalates. Loyalties are tested, alliances shift, and the game reaches its most dangerous level.

SEASON 5: THE END

The final chapter. Walter's empire crumbles, relationships shatter, and the ultimate price of his choices comes due. Remember his name.
